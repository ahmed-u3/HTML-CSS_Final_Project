Our Partnership Process

Discovery 1

The first step in our process is to meet with you to build an understanding of your business and the scope of your project.

  • The challenges you face
  • Your needs and requirements
  • Your goals and objectives
  • How we can help you

Deep Dive2

This is an in-depth session to get under the surface of your business to find out where you've been and where you're going next.

  • Reviewing your brand and business growth plans
  • Market, sector, and competitor research
  • Identifying and profiling your target audience
  • Analysis of your customer journey

Measure & Improve3

We help you turn data into insights to identify what parts of your project worked and what areas we can revisit and improve.

  • Organising planning days for data-driven strategy refinement
  • Conducting quarterly marketing meetings for ongoing development
  • Revisiting growth goals to align with market trends
  • Continuously measuring data to optimise marketing efforts
